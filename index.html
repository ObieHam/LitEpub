Literotica to EPUB

Convert Literotica stories and series to EPUB format for offline reading.

Supports: Single stories (/s/) and Series (/series/se/)

How to use:

  1. Copy a story or series URL from Literotica
  2. Paste it into the input field above
  3. Click "Convert to EPUB"
  4. Wait for the download to start
Note: This tool runs entirely in your browser using CORS proxies. Due to CORS restrictions, you may need to use a CORS proxy. Processing may take time for long series with multiple chapters.
